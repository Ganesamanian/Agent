# Escalation matrix

Low risk: routine clarification requests where policy and payment owner are clear.

Medium risk: refund complaints involving no-show disputes, unclear property policy, or incomplete payment evidence.

High risk: threats of legal action, regulator complaints, chargebacks, privacy rights requests, or repeated unresolved escalations.

For medium or high risk cases, the operator should create a documented handoff containing the timeline, payment owner, screenshots, and relevant policy excerpts.
