# Refunds and cancellations

If the guest cancelled inside the free-cancellation window and the booking was not later marked as a no-show, support should treat the charge as potentially refundable and gather the cancellation timestamp, property policy, and payment owner before responding.

If the booking is non-refundable, support should not promise a refund. The case can still be reviewed if there is duplicate charging, a property-side error, or evidence that the policy shown at booking was inconsistent.

When the property marked the reservation as a no-show after the guest claims to have cancelled, support must verify the timeline before declining the request. Timeline conflicts should be escalated.
