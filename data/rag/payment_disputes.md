# Payment disputes

The first question is who collected the money: Booking.com or the property. If the property charged the guest directly, support should collect the payment receipt, property message trail, and cancellation timeline before deciding on next steps.

Duplicate charges, chargebacks, and unclear pre-authorizations should be treated as medium-to-high risk. These cases should be escalated to finance if there is evidence of conflicting payment events.

Do not state that a pre-authorization is a final charge until the payment event is confirmed.
